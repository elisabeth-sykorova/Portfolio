Save Gregor

Roles: Game Design, Game Development, Art Direction, Font & UI

Introduction

Save Gregor is a small project developed in C++, SFML. It is a comedic adaptation of the ending (spoiler alert) of the book Metamorphosis by Franz Kafka.
This is a point and click mini-game, where your goal is to protect the randomly moving and turning cockroach Gregor from flying apples that otherwise harm him.
Though it is a small game, it came out exactly as it was concepted and designed, and its visuals are clean and polished.

Tools Used:

  • Adobe Photoshop
  • Aseprite
